# Marcus Ellested

Platform Engineer at Energinet, based in Odense, Denmark.

## What I do

I work on the platform team at Energinet, the operator of Denmark's electricity and gas
transmission grid. Day to day that means running and operating our Kubernetes platform,
writing Terraform for the infrastructure other teams build on, maintaining CI/CD pipelines,
and improving observability so we catch problems before they become incidents — with the
added weight of doing that for critical energy infrastructure.

## Stack

- **Platform:** Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform
- **Data:** PostgreSQL, MariaDB, MongoDB
